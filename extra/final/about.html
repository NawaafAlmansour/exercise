Our History

San Roque Medical Clinic is a non-stock, non-profit Healthcare Institution. It is licensed by the Department of Health and accredit by the Philippines Hospital Association and Philippine Health Insurance Corporation. It has (6) six major clinical departments and recognized by the different specialty socities for its Residency Training Program for Department of Internal Medicine, Pediatrics, Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynecology. SRMC is also accredited as Mother-Baby Friendly Hospital.
